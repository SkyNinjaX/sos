Quality Education

Causes:

  • Poor funding for rural schools
  • Inadequate teacher training
  • Flooding damaging school facilities

Solutions (Economics & Civic):

  • Encourage public-private partnerships for education funding
  • Community-led school rebuilding and maintenance initiatives
  • Scholarship and volunteer programs for rural learners

Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Causes:

  • Oil pollution affecting livelihoods
  • Poor road networks limiting access to markets
  • Lack of industrial diversification

Solutions (Geography, Economics, Commerce):

  • Develop sustainable road and bridge projects
  • Promote clean-tech and small-scale industries
  • Encourage youth entrepreneurship in renewable sectors

Sustainable Cities & Communities

Causes:

  • Unplanned urban growth
  • Inadequate waste management
  • Frequent flooding due to low elevation

Solutions (Geography, Economics, Commerce, Catering, Civic):

  • Promote flood-resilient housing designs
  • Establish local recycling businesses
  • Train youths in catering and urban food sustainability
  • Encourage civic participation in city planning

Climate Action

Causes:

  • Gas flaring and oil pollution
  • Deforestation and land degradation
  • High dependence on fossil fuels

Solutions (All Subjects):

  • Invest in renewable energy education
  • Encourage tree planting and mangrove restoration
  • Adopt strict laws against environmental pollution
